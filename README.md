

**Triple Cat – A Relaxing and Cute Matching Game!**

Welcome to *Triple Cat*, the ultimate stress-relief game with adorable kitties and a fresh, fun gameplay experience!

In *Triple Cat*, your goal is simple yet satisfying: find and match identical cats—whether it's pairs, trios, or even quadruples—depending on the game mode, and gently place them into boxes. With each match, you'll feel a burst of joy and cuteness!
